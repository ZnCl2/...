How to Buy Medical Cannabis on Darknet

10/30/2018

This site is for people who need personal-use amounts of medical marijuana but can’t purchase it legally where they live. If that describes you, darknet markets are an excellent option for you. We’ll show you how to safely and privately access hidden web markets online. Once there, you can buy high-quality medical marijuana online and pay anonymously using cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin.

What is a “Personal Use Amount” of Cannabis?

In most parts of the U.S., an ounce or less of cannabis flowers is considered to be a personal-use amount and is subject to lesser penalties under the law. If you don’t live in the U.S., you should find out what qualifies as a personal-use amount where you live.

The Darknet/Dark Web

When people talk about the “darknet” or “dark web,” they are generally referring to web sites on the Tor network. The U.S. Navy originally developed the Tor network to provide for highly secure, private internet communication. They later released the technology to the public for use by political dissidents living under oppressive governments. Today, a non-profit organization that promotes internet privacy and freedom administers the Tor network. For more information about the Tor network, see the Tor Project’s official web site here.

Darknet Markets

Darknet markets are web sites on the Tor network that connect buyers and sellers of a wide range of products and services. In most cases, the products and services are illegal in the buyers’ and/or sellers’ home countries. Sales of drugs represent the lion’s share of the business on most darknet markets.

The best markets have extensive features designed to protect buyers from ripoffs and scams. They have adopted some of the techniques used by Amazon and eBay to make shopping safer. For example, buyers rate vendors on darknet markets with star ratings and written comments. Buyers and sellers on darknet markets remain anonymous. To deal with the risks from anonymity, markets offer escrow systems. When buyers purchase using an escrow option, their money isn’t given to the vendor until the buyer has received his or her order.

To help you get started, this guide has all the information you need to safely buy your medical marijuana online.
