How to Order

You can order from us via email at zerocuts@secmail.pro. Send us an email or drop us a message with a list of the items you would like to buy and the address you would like it delivered to, we will reply within a few hours (9am-9pm British time or the next day if after 9pm) with the address to send funds to and the cost in pounds sterling and your chosen cryptocurrency. Once we have verified transaction with your TXID we will send your product out first class the same day (cut off 4pm mon-fri, 1pm sat). It will be packed in two layers of vacuum packing one regular and the other mylar, wiped down with ethanol and then put in an inconspicous package with a decoy where available.
