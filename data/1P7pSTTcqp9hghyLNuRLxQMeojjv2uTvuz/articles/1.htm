Is the Poland Responsible for Holocaust?



Some US and EU media giants wrote about new bill in Poland that forbids to accuse Polish nation and state of holocaust during WWII when it was occupied by German Nazi. nytimes.com:
"Yet it is also undeniable that Poles were directly or indirectly complicit in the crimes committed on their land and that Poles were guilty of anti-Jewish pogroms during and after the war. These are the facts of that terrible history, and the Poles, like all other nations conquered by Germany that became embroiled in the Nazi atrocities, have an obligation to the victims and to the future to seek the full truth, however painful." It is interesting interpretation of responsibility. One country occupies another, destroys its all government structures, prosecutes it's enemies,... and if few citizens of occupied country takes participation, that means, that occupied COUNTRY and NATION is responsible.
