Floflis OS

Operating system focused on security, privacy, modernity and newbie-friendly.
Based on Linux and ZeroNet, with HTML5 interface/apps.
Simple and easy to use, but advanced
Floflis combines both worlds: newbie-friendly, and advanced features.
It haves user levels (newbie, beginer, medium, advanced) and built-in computing courses, offering Open Badges as learning incentive.
Based on Linux
The same Linux/Debian apps you already use, can be imported and used on Floflis.
The security of Linux you know, but improved.
HTML5 UI and apps
Simple HTML5 UI, that is automatically updated using ZeroNet. Also, alongside Linux CLI programs and Debian apps/snaps, modern & lightweight HTML5 apps with auto-updates.
Chrome extensions/apps and Ethereum dApps can also be supported.
ZeroNet
The gentle version of deep web, with P2P distributed sites that supports user login without PHP.
Open zites on browser using zero:// protocol or use ZeroNet apps. You can also use built-in Namecoin to register .bit domains.
Easily create your first site or app
With Eventlr and FrameworkJS, you can easily create sites and apps with visual event sheets for programming without text-languages knowledge.
Just click a button, and your blank site will appear, and you just need to edit it.
Type faster
Floflis haves support and tutorials for Colemak keyboard layout.
Learn how to change your keyboard and use this faster keyboard layout.
Run windows, Android and Linux apps
With Anbox and Wine already set-up, you can run the apps from other OS you already use.
MacOS/iOS apps could also be supported.
Best setup for developers
Pre-installed Git, Git LFS, NodeJS, Electron, Cordova, Eventlr, FrameworkJS (with VueJS) and other dev tools.
It also haves a easy-to-use game engine that even a kid can use, that is like Construct 2/Eventlr: using visual event sheets.
Care about your identy
Create your profile, and verify all your works with Keybase/OpenPGP/Bitcoin signatures.
Share your Floflis profile link, and other users can see it.
Care about your works
Use Namecoin to register "copyrights".
Soon: copyright app that uses Namecoin's RPC API and other copyright services.
Chat with privacy
With Riot.im, a Discord alternative
Soon: totally decentralized Riot.im on ZeroNet
Quantum-resistant money
With Quantum Resistant Ledger, a Bitcoin alternative that is designed to be even more resistant to quantum computing
Soon: quantum resistant user identies and sites/apps
Connect to your family, friends and neighbors
With CJDNS/YouNet/ZeroNet, you can access offline sites and send offline messages.
I'm not kidding about offline messages! You don't need to have a Internet service to browse sites, download new content and send messages to your family and other people. Just connect to an online neighbor thought the same open WiFi network (neighbor don't need to let you access its private Internet connection, you connect to an internetless WiFi that haves online users), using YouNet/ZeroNet/CJDNS
Open Badges
Show to other users on your profile, what are your efforts and achievements when using Floflis and its apps.
Open Badges is a amazing technology. Other users can verify if the badges are really given by Floflis, and if you really did the requirement to earn it.
Best OS for videos
Think, project, record, transmit, edit, compile, and distribute/publish.
With Kdenlive editor, OBS Studio (for screenrecording and livestreamming), Video Script Maker and KxoVid P2P video sharing site, give a next level to your video production.
