About

isolinks was created in 2014 on the idea that Microsoft software should be available to everyone and not just msdn subscribers. I became an msdn subscriber through the BizSpark program to gain access to the msdn portal for free. I sold all of the product keys I received through the program, downloaded the most popular software, uploaded it to mega and hosted it for the convenience of anyone who may be searching for it. New software as it comes out will be added to the site just as long as I have access to the msdn portal. If there is a particular .iso image you're looking for that's not on here please contact me via Bitmessage and I'll get it on here as soon as possible for you or maybe for others that might be searching for it.
