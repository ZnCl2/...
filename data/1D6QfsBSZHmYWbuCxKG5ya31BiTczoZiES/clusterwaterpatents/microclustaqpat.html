Box-type water purification/magnetization drinking device
CN204058161


The utility model relates to a box-type water purification/magnetization drinking device which comprises a box body, and a water inlet pipe and a water outlet pipe which are arranged on the box body, wherein a magnetization water purifier and two sets of filter units are arranged in the box body; two filter elements of the filter units form one group and are connected in parallel; water inlets of the first set of filter units are connected in parallel and connected to the water inlet pipe to be connected with a tapping water pipe; water is subjected to primary filter purification by the first set of filter units; water outlets of the first set of filter units are respectively correspondingly communicated with water inlets of the second set of filter units; the water is subjected to secondary filter purification by the second set of filter units; water outlets of the second set of filter units are connected in parallel and connected to a water inlet of the magnetization water purifier; the water is subjected to hard magnetization treatment by the magnetization water purifier; and a water outlet of the magnetization water purifier is connected to the water outlet pipe and communicated with a water inlet pipe of a water tap. The box-type water purification/magnetization drinking device scientifically utilizes the parallel purification/magnetization water purification function, has high water output, and can maintain beneficial minerals and necessary minor elements for the human body in water; after being subjected to hard magnetization treatment, the water is weakly alkaline of microcluster, has high oxygen content, and can reduce the free radicals in the human body and inhibit tumor cells from propagation and diffusion; after being drunk for a long time, the water can change the acidic constitution of the human body to achieve acid-base balance, thereby being beneficial to good health of people.

Novel multifunctional ceramic filter element
CN104176856



The invention discloses a novel multifunctional ceramic filter element. The novel multifunctional ceramic filter element comprises a ceramic filter tube filled with water purification filter materials, a plastic lower cover, a plastic upper cover and a stainless steel filter screen, wherein the water purification filter materials filled in the ceramic filter element comprise 10% of activated carbon, 20% of tourmaline, 20% of medical stone, 20% of Muyu stone, 10% of germanite, 10% of KDF and 10% of negative potential; the widths of the plastic covers on both ends of the ceramic filter element are 2-3cm; and the filter screen inside the ceramic filter element is a 200-mesh 304 stainless steel filter cover. The ceramic filter element is filled with the activated carbon, tourmaline, medical stone, Muyu stone, germanite, KDF, negative potential and other filter materials, the outlet water has mineral minor elements, weak alkalinity, negative potential, microcluster and other functions; the upper and lower plastic covers of the ceramic filter element are 2-3cm wide and enhance the safety performance of the two ends of the ceramic filter element, thereby avoiding degumming, rupture and damage; and the water outlet end inside the ceramic filter element is provided with the 200-mesh stainless steel filter screen, so that the ceramic filter element can not be blocked to influence the water yield, and has long service life.

Microcluster water preparation device and method
CN103466756

 

The invention discloses a microcluster water preparation device comprising a non-magnetisms-conducting container containing water. A thin metal body is arranged in the container. A magnet exciting coil is arranged outside the container. A control device supplies the alternating current to the magnet exciting coil. The thin metal body is placed in the range of magnetic lines of an alternating magnetic field generated by the magnet exciting coil. The thin metal body is provided with discharging grooves and/or discharging holes. The discharging grooves and/or the discharging holes generate a discharge phenomenon in water, so that weak electrolysis is conducted on water, and microcluster water is generated. The thin metal body is of a shape of one or a combination of more than one of a slice, a cylinder, a ring and a basin. The microcluster water preparation device is simple in structure and high in production efficiency. According to a microcluster water preparation method, structural matching of the non-magnetisms-conducting container containing processed water, the thin metal body located in the non-magnetisms-conducting container and the magnet exciting coil exerting the magnetic field is adopted. According to the prepared microcluster water, a value of the 170NMR peak width at half height of a water sample is small, and the prepared microcluster water is stable in nature and fine and smooth in taste.

Hydrogen-rich microcluster water and manufacturing method thereof
CN102992416

The invention relates to a hydrogen-rich microcluster water and a manufacturing method thereof. 0.1-63 parts of ultrafine ground 10nm-1mm granules of Huhaiyan mineral, 0-12 parts of assistant, 1-50 parts of adhesive and 0.1-10 parts of magnesium powder are mixed, formed, and made into sheets and blocks; and when water and mixture are mixed in a solid-to-liquid ratio of 1:10, after 15 minutes, the determination indicates that the pH value of water is greater than 7, and the oxygen-17 NMR (nuclear magnetic resonance) is less than 79, and thus, the water is weakly alkaline hydrogen-rich microcluster water. The technique comprises the following steps: making natural Guhaiyan into a cup, pot or any other vessel, or making natural Guhaiyan into small blocks, sheets or granules (1cm above), or pulverizing into micro/nano powder; and adding a certain amount of adhesive and water in a mixer provided with a stirrer and a thermometer, evenly mixing, and finally, forming. Water is filled in a container, or natural Guhaiyan or a formed substance containing Guhaiyan is added into water in a solid-to-liquid ratio of (1-9):(91-99) to prepare the weakly alkaline hydrogen-rich microcluster water of which the pH value is 7.2-9.2 and the 17-O NMR is 45-79, thereby satisfying the basic requirements for health of people. The product has obvious effects of sterilization, disinfection and deodorization.

Water magnetizer
CN202430043

The center of the body is provided with a water through hole penetrating through the body lengthwise, two sides of the water through hole are provided with two magnet cavities respectively, the end cover comprises a cover body and a joint pipe, the N-magnet is fixed in one of the magnet cavities, and the S-magnet is fixed in the other magnet cavity. Multi-dimensional composite energy active material and preparation method thereof
CN101891264

The invention discloses a multi-dimensional composite energy active material and a preparation method thereof. The material is composed of Muyu stone, tourmaline, medical stone, germanite, parmesan rock, igneous rock and ceramic clay. The preparation method of the multi-dimensional composite energy active material comprises the following steps: firstly, immersing parmesan rock powders with edible ethylic acid and then carrying out calcination, wherein the parmesan rock powders are crushed into nanometer grains; then carrying out high-speed stir and even mixing on the immersed parmesan rock powders and the balance of the nanometer grains of components and forming ball grains; finally, sintering the formed ball grains for 2-4 hours at the temperature of 800-1200 DEG C and carrying out disinfection by ultraviolet light and sterile packaging to obtain the material.; The multi-dimensional composite energy active material in the invention can be applied to various water purification machines, water-quality filters and health cups.
