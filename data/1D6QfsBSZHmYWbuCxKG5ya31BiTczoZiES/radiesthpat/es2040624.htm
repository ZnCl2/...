ES2040624
Electromotive power generator for therapeutic uses

Inventor:  RAMOS FERNANDEZ FELICISIMO

Abstract -- Electric motor generator for therapeutic use consisting of a base plate made of earthling material, preferably triangular with curved sides that are concave towards the interior, in which a variable amount of magnets are inserted, situated alternately with their poles differently set, and positioned so that there is an apex at the centre, set so that the arcs have concentric conferences with respective zinc plates, making the electrolyte of the constituent battery. This plate is supported by an apex and when it is turned towards this parallel to the affected area an electrical field is induced whose frequency corresponds to the number of magnets set in each concentric arc. In order to store and transport it there are iron plates that make screens that prevent permanent magnetic fields from being created while the generator isn't being used.
