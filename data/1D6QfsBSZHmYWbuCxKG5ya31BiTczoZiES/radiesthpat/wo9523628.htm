DEVICE FOR FORMING AN ENERGY FIELD 

Inventor:  TOLSTOI VLADIMIR STEPANOVICH
Also published as: AU7865694       

Abstract -- The proposed device for forming an energy field comprises at least one collector (1) made up of a plurality of energy sources (2) arranged along a closed line and separated by gaps, with a central open area (6). The energy sources (2) are interconnected and can move in relation to each other.
