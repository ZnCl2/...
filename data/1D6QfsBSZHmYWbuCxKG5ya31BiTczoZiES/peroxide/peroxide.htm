Hydrozone: 30 Vol H2O2 Solution

One cc of Hydrogen Peroxide (15 Vol) diluted with 150 cc distilled water containing 2 cc C.P. Sulfuric Acid decolorizes at least 15 cc of a solution containing 5.665 gr C.P. potassium permanganate per liter of distilled water.

One cc of Hydrozone decolorizes at least 30 cc of the above solution of K-permanganate.

One fluid ounce of Hydrozone being mixed with one fluid ounce of distilled water will give two fluid ounces of 15 Vol H2O2 solution.

Hydrozone yields 30 times its volume of Nascent Oxygen (near to the condition of Ozone). It retains it germicidal and curative power indefinitely when stored with ordinary care.

It is slightly acid and should never be made neutral before using it.
