Remarks:

(1) A 3 diode 'pair' off-set is included -- which is switchable; no diode, 1-diode, 2 diodes, or 3 diodes. Each diode pair introduces about 0.7 volts of off-set. pairs are used in case polarity reversal occurs in some tests.
