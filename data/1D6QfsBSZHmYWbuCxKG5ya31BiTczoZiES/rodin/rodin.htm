Rodin Coil

A Rodin Coil is a doughnut-shaped form of wires that increases the strength of a magnetic field. The shape of this coil is beneficial because it allows an easier distribution of magnetic fields to all the parts of a cicuit.[1] It is credible in produces more efficient electronic devices because of its ability to distribute magentic fields.
