info

 These proxies/gateways allow non-ZeroNet users to access content from ZeroNet.
  • howto:
    • the column "example" below
      shows the url you can share
      with "the outsiders" for e.g. this page you are viewing right now
  • Keep in mind:
    • It's always better to run your own ZeroNet client
    • These are NOT "official" proxies, but operated by (any kind of) users.
    • Those users could be friendly,
      they could not have resources to run them in the future,
      they could also be working for a 3-letter state agency,
      they can be able to access your account,
      ...
      see also section "specific sites" at: ZeroSecurity
    • If you've to use one of the proxies below, use at least a virtual machine.
  • If you know other proxies:
    • please contact me
