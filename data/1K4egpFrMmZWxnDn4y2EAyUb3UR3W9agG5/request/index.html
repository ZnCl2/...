What is a RushWallet Fundraiser?
Our fundraising tool allows you to raise funds for your crowd-funding initiative or to pool money together from multiple people.
Create a new Fundraiser
Click on settings/tools icon in the top bar and then click on Fundraiser Manager. Select create new fundraiser and enter the name, description, and amount needed. DO NOT FORGET YOUR FUNDRAISER ID! Write it down and store it in a safe place, this ID is required if you ever need to restore your fundraiser.
Open Fundraiser
Click on the fundraiser name to open and view the details of your fundraiser.
Share your Fundraiser
You can share your fundraiser URL on your website, blog, and social media channels or use the facebook or twitter share icons.
 Open Fundraiser Wallet
This wallet is separate from your original wallet and stores your funds associated with your fundraiser. Ensure you never share your wallet’s URL.
 Sweep Fundraiser
One click sweep ability allows you to sweep the bitcoins from your fundraiser into your main RushWallet. The progress meter will continue to show the total bitcoin amount contributed.
 Delete Fundraiser
Deleting your fundraiser will simply remove it from your manager. You can never permanently delete your fundraiser.
Load your existing Fundraiser
To retrieve your wallet, select fundraiser manager in the settings/tools icon. Select load existing fundraiser and input your Fundraiser ID.
For more information go to Kryptokit.com/rush
What is a RushWallet Payment Request?
This RushWallet tool allows you to easily create and manage requests for payments.
Create your Payment Request
Click on settings/tools icon in the top bar and then click on Payment Request Manager. Select create new payment request and enter the name, description, and amount. DO NOT FORGET YOUR REQUEST ID! Write it down and store it in a safe place, this ID is required if you ever need to restore your fundraiser.
Open Payment Request
Click on the request name to open and view the details of your payment request.
Send your Payment Request
Email or text your payment request URL to your client.
  Open Payment Request Wallet
This wallet is separate from your original wallet and stores your funds associated with your payment request. Ensure you never share your wallet’s URL.
  Sweep Payment Request
One click sweep ability allows you to sweep the bitcoins from your Payment Request into your main RushWallet. The progress meter will continue to show the total bitcoin amount collected.
  Delete Payment Request
Deleting your payment request will simply remove it from your manager. You can never permanently delete your payment request.
Load your existing Payment Request
To retrieve your request, select Payment Request Manager in the settings/tools icon. Select load existing payment request and input your Payment Request ID.
