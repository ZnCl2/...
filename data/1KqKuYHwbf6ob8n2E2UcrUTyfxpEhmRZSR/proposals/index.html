Call for Proposals

There are many ways for you to share your knowledge at the Decentralized Web Summit. Our focus is on teaching rather than promoting a product or project.  We are looking for great, interactive sessions in technology, law, policy, governance, the arts and other use cases of the Decentralized Web.

Before submitting a proposal, please register to attend.  Only registered participants will be considered.

Some ways you can share your knowledge:

 

SCIENCE FAIR -- at the Opening Night Party, Tuesday, July 31 from 6-9 p.m.

Each team will have a small cocktail table, a sign, and great WIFI.  Bring your laptops or props and share your knowledge one-on-one during the Opening Night Party.  It’s a chance for attendees to get their first understanding of you and your project.

 

SCIENCE FAIR PROPOSALS ARE NOW CLOSED.  SCIENCE FAIR ORGANIZER, MEGAN WONG, WILL CONFIRM YOUR PARTICIPATION IF YOU ARE SELECTED.

  1. Have your registered through Eventbrite?

  2. Have you filled out this form so we can make your Science Fair sign and have your table ready?

  3. Have you sent us your photo, preferred title and short bio so we can include you on the web site as a presenter?

 

LIGHTNING TALKS — at the Decentralized Web Summit, Wednesday-Thursday, August 1-2nd, 9 a.m. -5 p.m.

Short stand-up talks, 3 minutes long with a minute of questions.  Slides are optional.

 

LIGHTNING TALKS ARE NOW CLOSED.  LIGHTNING TALK ORGANIZER, JOHN LIGHT, WILL BE IN TOUCH REGARDING YOUR STATUS AND SCHEDULE:

  1. Have you signed up in the Eventbrite Ticketing process? 

  2. Be sure to send him your photo, preferred title and short bio so we can include you on the web site as a presenter.

 

PANELS, WORKSHOPS, TALKS OR SOMETHING CREATIVE — at the Decentralized Web Summit, August 1-2nd, 9 a.m. -5 p.m.

PROPOSALS FOR WORKSHOPS, PANELS & TALKS ARE NOW CLOSED.  TRACK ORGANIZERS WILL CONTACT YOU REGARDING YOUR STATUS AND SCHEDULE.
