Culture of Decentralization and Emerging Norms

This roundtable will focus on emergent norms and culture in the decentralized space and its implications for the shape of the future of the Internet. This is a discussion that is sorely lacking in today’s superficial dialogue around lambos and cryptokittes, etc. We will identify and analyze the social context, mediums of communications, key stakeholders, and primary drivers by and in which these norms are currently articulated, normalized, adopted and institutionalized. The goal is to develop a solid and nuanced understanding of the state of the culture of decentralization today, where we would like to go, and how to get there, as we continue to build together.

Law, Policy, and Governance
Primavera De Filippi, Constance Choi, Kerry Maeve Sheehan, Paul Lindner, Kevin Silverman
