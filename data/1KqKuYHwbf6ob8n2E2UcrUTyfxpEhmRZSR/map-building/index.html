Decentralized Map Building (A Field Simulation)

Poor internet connection, old and slow computers, and viruses, humidity? Welcome to our jungle. In this hands-on workshop we will be making an online map while simulating the real-world conditions faced by the communities who use the tools in the field. Older machines, slow-to-no Internet, etc will not be a problem for Mapeo, Digital Democracy's decentralized tool that allows map makers to work off-line and sync their data later.

Outcomes? You'll build deeper understanding of the technical hurdles faced by communities that could be end users of your tools, and how the environment dictates specific considerations about how the tools themselves are built.

Please bring your LAPTOP, if you want to participate in the simulation.
