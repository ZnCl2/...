Irks and quirks

Time to read: 2 min
July 27, 2017

Today we encounter the rising cost of consumer goods for political bullshit reasons.

The price of everthing we buy (or sell) is dictated to us by industry.

When the cost of corn goes up- the cost of ethanol rises and transportation costs rise.
When the cost of trucking gas rises- the cost of food goes up.

Its not a secret.
The market wants you to think that “it is”.

But when EYE EXAMS go UP UP UP UP UP and UP at the local store, you have to suspect something.
This isnt market competition- its consumer THEFT.

I looked at similar stores with in-store eye exams.
The price was about half of what this idiot is charging..so why is he charging so much?

Its not convienience.
Its to make a buck off of the stupid locals willing to pay that price.
If you drive 90 mins away- the price is far less.

And yet watch- by september this guy will want $200 for a contact lens exam….

There is still an ongoing Tv special on some of the broadcast-band sub channels..
Its called “american greed”.

-Worth a watch.
