Donate to the NinjaBeats movement

There are two forms of financial donations you can make to NinjaBeats: Direct and Mixed.

In a nutshell

  • Direct pot: Anything in the direct pot we use to fund new improvements and keep us motivated. If you are unsure which donation pot to donate to, choose this one (the direct pot).
  • Mixed pot: At least half of the money in the mixed pot gets forwarded to the direct pot and the mixed pot is used for emergency funds.

"Direct pot"

All revenue recieved before public launch goes to this pot. If you are unsure which donation pot to donate to, choose this one (the direct pot).

  • All donations recieved here go directly to NinjaBeats to help us fund new improvements and keep us motivated
  • Absolutely none of the funds recieved from direct donations go to music artists, labels or other third-parties - it all goes directly to us and the NinjaBeats project




"Mixed pot"

This is a bit confusing I know, eventually I'll clear it up and simplify it, or I might scrap it entirely before the public launch... I'm not sure yet.

  • All donations recieved in the "mixed pot" are mixed in with ad revenue
  • The money in the mixed pot gets split and sent accordingly, sometimes only under certain conditions:
    • At least 50% of all money recieved in the mixed pot (from both "mixed pot" donations and ad revenue) gets forwarded to our direct donations pot to help us fund new improvements and keep us motivated
    • In the case that an artist politely requests their ad revenue (with verification that it is genuinely the artist in question requesting the ad revenue), the percentage of ad revenue obtained from that artist will be forwarded directly to them if deemed reasonable. This is basically our method of staying ethical.
      • If the artist or anyone associated with that artist (such as a label or their lawyer(s)) files a copyright claim before politely requesting their ad revenue, we will irreversibly forward all their ad revenue to our direct donations pot and will continue forwarding any future ad revenue percentage from said artist until the copyright claims are lifted.
    • Donation money recieved in this pot gets split equally across all artists, after at least 50% of it is forwarded to our "direct pot" as stated earlier.
