Red Liberty

Red Liberty is my (mainly) political blog, it's been online (clearnet) for about 3 years now and it's my first eepsite. I was determined to host it over I2P after reading an article in 2600 about I2P. "Socialism or Barbarism, Liberty or Death! Freedom is always the freedom of dissent!"

Marxists Internet Archive

This is the unofficial I2P mirror of the Marxists Internet Archive (marxists.org). This site is HUGE! A complete mirror of the Marxists Internet Archive, it has over 400GB of files! The MIA is a scholarly/ academic resource. In the future it may in time become an official mirror, but that depends on if and when I am able to move it to a data center.
