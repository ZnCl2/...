We need your help






We need help sorting media and creating new collections of media.

If you are the type that likes to organize or if you have a creative knack for mixtaping together interesting collections then read on....







We reimburse you for your work with unlimited access to our collection.

You receive credit which you can use to order anything you want from our catalogs, including media that isn't available to the general public.







What do I actually have to do?

Mediarians - You would be doing things like taking 3,000 music albums and sorting them into categories or a taking a list of 400 TV shows and finding how many seasons there are for each. It is tedious work, but we value accuracy over speed so it is not high pressure.

Curators - We need people to use what is available in the Catalogs and Lists and create new collections of media that customers would like to see, read or listen to. For this kind of work we don't tell you what we need done, instead we are counting on your original ideas to come up with something we like.
