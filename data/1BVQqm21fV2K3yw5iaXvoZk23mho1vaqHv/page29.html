DMD 525














What is this?





Digital Media Delivery (DMD) is an attempt by some people to make all digital media available to everyone, everywhere.



















How are you going to do this?





We are going to employ a network of people and leverage the internet to distribute media at a reasonable price.



















Why are you doing this?





Because it is the right thing to do.



















Who are you?





Just another small, anonymous group of people working together - Wish you were here.



















What about copyright?





We support copyright, but current copyright law is outdated and corrupt and needs to be changed to reflect the new digital age we live in.
