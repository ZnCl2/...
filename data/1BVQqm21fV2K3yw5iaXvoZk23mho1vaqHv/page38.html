About These Statistics






















Catalogs - Media is fully organized and categorized






















Lists - Only basic sorting of media has been done














































Magazines, Music Videos and Music Concerts are each one set of






















media that have all been sorted in two ways - by Cluster and by Title






















By Cluster






















Media that is similar by name or source is grouped together






















By Title






















The names or titles of the media are sorted alphabetically






































































* but... there are file duplicates, especially in Movies, 2%-4%






















depending on media. So actual amount of original media is smaller






















than listed.














































** exact amount of media may be a wee bit different than what is






















shown here - we did, however, give it our best shot.
