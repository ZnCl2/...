What is MASSmedia Local?

MASSmedia Local is a service of DMD which delivers digital media using a network of people in one local area.







You can help us and make money at the same time!

You can work whenever you want as much as you want.

People who are Distributors form the backbone of the network and often have other jobs. They work for MASSmedia when they want to make some extra money. We also need Partners, who can work on a more full time basis feeding media to Distributors.







What do I actually have to do?

We are trying to distribute media like movies, TV, music, books etc... as widely as possible, to as many people as possible. So what we need are people who know other people who would like to buy our media. By joining MASSmedia you would form you own network of people who would buy our media through you.







This is a new service and only available in a few places....

So if you got a card from us or were directed here some other way it means you should be living in an area where MASSmedia is operating.
