About OneSearch

OneSearch indexes more than 800 sites and 10,000 individual pages. Some are duplicates, but this is still more than any other ZeroNet search engine.

If you want your site indexed, then make sure that at least one other site links to it. You might want to add your site to 0List.

Previously search queries were sent to a server, but now they are all done entirely within ZeroNet.

Advanced Features

Find indexed pages on a given domain. Example: site:0list.bit

Find backlinks for a given domain. Example: link:0list.bit

You can even combine them.
