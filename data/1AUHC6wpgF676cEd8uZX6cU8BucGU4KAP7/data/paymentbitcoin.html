比特幣是先進安全快捷低廉的電子全球支付。點對點加密貨幣，無需經過任何金融機構，可在網上由一人直接付款給另一人。

  • 加密貨幣交易平台（一般從交易平台開始較為簡單）：
    1. 註冊和登錄
    2. 充值
    3. 買入 BTC ...
    4. 提現 BTC ...（向多成付款）
  • 加密貨幣錢包（比特幣除了在交易平台購買，很多錢包都內置以信用卡購買的功能，如 BRD, Coinbase, Mycelium, Trust, Liquid Pro, ShapeShift, Bittrex 等）：
    1. 打開錢包
    2. 點擊 "發送貨幣"
    3. 輸入、抄入或掃描（付款）比特幣地址
    4. 輸入 金額
    5. 確認 付款
  • 說明：
    • 付款價值：可參考 BlockChain 的「交易時價值」，將鼠標放在相應交易的收款數值處，即可顯示；
    • 貨幣漲跌：這只是個轉帳工具，其漲跌對以支付診金為目的者沒什麼關係，不需屯幣（時價。銀行匯出$100，與$100兌換比特幣匯出，除了匯款手續費非常低廉之外沒什麼不同）。也不需紙錢包、離線錢包、密碼（因手機已有密碼或指紋鎖）；
    • 正確的手續費：
      • 查看最新區塊的最低手續費（例如，你可以到Blockchain.info網站點擊最新的區塊。滾動到底部查看區塊中的一些交易，因為交易費最低的交易通常在底部），然後把你自己的手續費設定的稍微比這高一點；
      • 很多錢包或交易所目前並不支持自定義手續費，這將造成浪費。可考慮將你的幣轉移到能夠自定義手續費的錢包（如Electrum和Bitcoin Core等。當然這就要承受幣值下降的風險）；
  • 比特幣相關網站：https://bitcoin.org

Bitcoin is advanced, safe, fast and low fee electronic global payments. Point-to-point digital currency, without going through any financial institution, you can pay online directly from one person to another person.

  • Cryptocurrency Trading Platform (generally from the Bitcoin trading platform started relatively simple):
    1. Register and login
    2. Recharge
    3. Buy BTC ...
    4. Withdrawals BTC ... (Pay to DuoSuccess)
  • Cryptocurrency Wallet (in addition to Bitcoin purchases on the trading platform, many wallets have built-in credit card purchase features such as BRD, Coinbase, Mycelium, Trust, Liquid Pro, ShapeShift, Bittrex, etc.):
    1. Open your wallet
    2. Click on the "send coins" button
    3. Input into the copy or scan (payment) Bitcoin address
    4. Enter the amount
    5. Confirm payment
  • Interpretation:
    • Payment Value: You can refer to the BlockChain "transaction value", place the mouse at the value of the receipt of the corresponding transaction.

      • Check the minimum fee for the latest block (For example, you can click on the latest block on the Blockchain.info website. Scroll to the bottom to see some of the transactions in the block, because the transaction fee is the lowest at the bottom of the transaction), and then set your own fee slightly higher.
