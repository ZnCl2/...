    Since this is a bug within ZeroMe, there is nothing I can do(I only own this hub, not ZeroMe itself).
    If you want to post images or upload a profile image, you'll have to either temporarily use firefox(or any another browser) or temporarily disable the canvas fingerprint protection, which you can do by going to
    about:config
    , searching for
    privacy.resistFingerprinting
    and setting it to
    false
    , while uploading the image.
    Do not
    forget to turn this setting back to
    true
    once you're done!
    Explanation
    :
    ZeroMe will redraw each image before actually uploading it, by accsessing the canvas element, but any attempt to do that is ignored by the Tor Browser. Since ZeroMe does not receive an error, it just assumes that the image has been successfully redrawn and continues to upload the 'redrawn' image, which is still empty.
    This is why your uploaded images are just white images.
    I will remove this section, once this bug gets resolved.
