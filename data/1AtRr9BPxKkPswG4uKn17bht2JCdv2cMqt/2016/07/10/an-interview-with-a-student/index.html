An interview with a student

July 10, 2016July 10, 2016 user 0 Comment

An interview with a local student about Dawah literature.

Ali is a 25-year old student in Deir Az-Zour. He speaks to us about how his life changed after reading some of the Dawah pamplets published by the Himmah Library publishing house.

Interviewer: How was life before you read the Islamic Dawah published by us?

Ali: I was a bad person. I sinned a lot and was not aware of Tawheed and the Ahkam ash-Shariah. Alhamdulillah the brothers came by me one day and handed me a pamphlet. I read it and read things which none of our parents or Imams taught us before.
