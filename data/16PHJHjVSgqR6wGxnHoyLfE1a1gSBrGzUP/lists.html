Subscriptions to Postfix-related mailing lists

Note: postings from non-members are silently discarded.

  • To stop list mail, send mail to majordomo@postfix.org with content (not subject) one or more of the following commands:

    unsubscribe postfix-announce
    unsubscribe postfix-users
    unsubscribe postfix-devel

    If you receive mail at an alternate address, specify it at the end of the command:

    unsubscribe postfix-listname user@example.com

  • To receive list mail, send mail to majordomo@postfix.org with content (not subject) one or more of the following commands:

    subscribe postfix-announce
    subscribe postfix-users
    subscribe postfix-devel

    If you receive mail at an alternate address, specify it at the end of the command:

    subscribe postfix-listname user@example.com

  • postfix-announce@postfix.org: READ-ONLY list for announcements of Postfix releases and updates.

    Open subscription, no posting.

  • postfix-users@postfix.org: for discussions about using Postfix: questions, problem reports, or feature requests.

    Open subscription, unmoderated posting by members only.

  • postfix-devel@postfix.org: for people who design, implement or maintain Postfix source code. NOT for questions, problem reports and feature requests.

    Open subscription, unmoderated posting by members only.
