ABOUT

I am Kevin Froman, a 19 year old developer and security researcher. I am passionate about security, privacy, and improving society using software.

Some of my work/research includes whitespace steganography software, a decentralized peer-to-peer pastebin, an interesting timing attack on anonymity networks and various exploits in software such as torrent clients, I2P, Freenet, and network router interfaces.
