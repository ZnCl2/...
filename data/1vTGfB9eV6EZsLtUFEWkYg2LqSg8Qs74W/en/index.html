PERYX

Private Digital Currency

Peryx is cash for a connected world. It’s fast, private, and secure. With Peryx, you are your own bank. You can spend safely, knowing that others cannot see your balances or limit your activity.

GET STARTED

Why Peryx is different

Peryx is social

Peryx is freely distributed to low income people and to groups contributing to the society therefore it helps the economic and social progress.

Peryx is independent

Each user has full control over his money. Problems of the traditional financial system such as hyperinflation, confiscations, accounts freezing, wealth targeting, capital controls do not affect Peryx.

Peryx is secure

Peryx is a decentralized network consisted of simple users. Transactions are confirmed by distributed users consensus and then immutably recorded on the blockchain. This kind of security is not based on any central authority or a third party.

Peryx is private

Peryx uses ring signatures, ring confidential transactions, and stealth addresses to obfuscate the origins, amounts, and destinations of all transactions. Transactions on Peryx blockchain can't be associated with a particular user or the identity of a real person.
