Open Source Writing

I was looking around reddit a few weeks ago, when I came across a sub-reddit called WritingPrompts. here people were either starting short stories or adding to them, often with hilarious results. As the structure of reddit didn't really lend it's self to this kind of writing, I decided to write one Here, I would like to port it to ZeroNet in the near future.

Users of this system can add more sections to any story, they can also create a new story.
