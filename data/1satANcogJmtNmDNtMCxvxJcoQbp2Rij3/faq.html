About Us

Kill with me is a crime collaboration platform where a game is posted and players/user have the ability to interact either by stopping or helping the event to happen (end result)

How to earn?

Every time a new user join the pool 75% of the registration fee will be distributed evenly to all members and the 25% will be use as operating expenses

How to earn more?

Each game are related to extortion once the target pays the demand money to a designated address. All participating players will receive 75% of the total loot and it will be divided by percentage share. For example, if you share 7%, you will get 75% * demand money

When will I get paid?

Payment from member joining will be sent in 24-48 hours. You can check the blockchain for auditing. All records regarding payment are stored on the blockchain.

For bet game, price will be sent to the public key registered only.
