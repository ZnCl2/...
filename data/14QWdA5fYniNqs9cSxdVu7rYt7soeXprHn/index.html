BitcoinPrice

This site is for fetching the BTC price through ZeroNet programmatically. The price is updated every 15 minutes by a script and uses averages of the major bitcoin exchanges to determine the current price.

Instructions

You can programmatically fetch the price.json file and parse its contents using any JSON compliant library.
