Flyman's Transfer: Main page.

Comments.

This is my experiment to use ZeroNet to share files I have. So how do I imagine this. This is main site. It supposed to be small and it contains links and comments for number of other sites (I think about 5 to 10) that will contain something I want to share. Those secondary sites (slots) might be large, so be careful not to load something big you do not want.

Instructions.

  • Look at comments to slot.
  • If you want files in some slot, then click on this slot to download zite-slot.
  • Find files on your hdd at [ZeroNetPath]\data\[Slot Addr]\Files\ and copy them somewhere.
  • Seed it as long as you like.
  • Delete it, because I will eventually reuse this slot for some other files you might not interested in (I intend to make warning before it).
