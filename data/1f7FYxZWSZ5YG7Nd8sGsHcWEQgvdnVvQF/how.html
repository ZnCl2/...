Getting your zite added to RVRE

Getting your zite added to RVRE is easy, but does take some time. Just follow these simple instructions:

  1. Add the following to the <head> element of your zite's index.html:
    <meta name="keywords" content="[YOUR KEYWORDS SEPARATED BY COMMAS HERE!]">
    For example, here's how it looks on this zite:
    <meta name="keywords" content="rvre,bwoi,search,engine,free,simple">
    This is very important! Without these, RVRE cannot correctly index your zite and you may end up with incorrect/missing tags!
  2. Make sure your zite is being seeded by bit.surf; if it isn't, simply visit it using that proxy.
    This proxy is used by both RVRE and other search engines to get new zites and update existing zites.
  3. Either mail me or wait for the index to be updated (see the footer for when that happens); if enough people mail me before the index needs updating, I will update it manually.
After this, we'll work out the rest and verify your zite! It's really straightforward and worry-free.
