ZeroMovies

This site accesses clearnet apis in order to deliver content. Do not enter if you wish to keep all of your traffic on ZeroNet. The sites and resources accessed are: http://api.themoviedb.org and http://image.tmdb.org (poster/backdrop API), http://ajax.googleapis.com (jquery host), http://vodlocker.to (streaming embed host), http://www.imdb.com (fetch IMDB id), along with whatever source is used to host the videos themselves (namely google drive and thevideo.me among others). Please be aware your network traffic and browser may be tracked by these sites.
The dereferer previously used has gone offline. To get the IMDB search results, please disable CORS. You can do that with this firefox extension or this chrome extension.
