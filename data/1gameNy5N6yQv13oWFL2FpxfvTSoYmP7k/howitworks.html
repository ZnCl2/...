Send the exact amount specified for the game

If you send more or less than what is specified, whatever sent will be ignored by the game and the received tx will be considered a donation.

Ticking block game:

The block games are fully automated. The ticking block is a simple jackpot that wins 90% of all payments of the ticking block .
When the tick block reaches 36 blocks (About 6 hours) without receiving a transaction to the game address, whoever has the last transaction paid to the address for the last block will receive the jackpot.

Fee's: 90% of all payments to the winner. House takes 10% to pay for tx fee's, and game support...

Daily/Weekly/Monthly block games:

The block games are fully automated. These are multiplier/jackpot type games.


Multiplier: You get paid 1.2 * the cost of the game. For example, suppose you paid 0.01 BTC to play the daily time , if there are enough players after you, you will be paid back 0.012 BTC.

Jackpot: When the time reaches the amount of blocks, whoever has the last transaction paid to the address for the last block will receive the jackpot.

Fee's: 70% of every payment for the Daily/Weekly/Monthly block games will go to the multiplier and 20% to the jackpot. House takes 10% to pay for tx fee's, and game support...
