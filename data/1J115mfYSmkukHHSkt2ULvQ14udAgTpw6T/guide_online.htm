Guide: How to play Bloody Roar 2 Online


The best to run on your Pc fighting game - now online. With our installer you will play with players from the whole world.

How to install and play Bloody Roar 2 Online?
First thing to do is download:
 ePSXe with Br2Online by PFTM v1.6

Extract 4 part of BR2Online, and run BR2Online.exe first, Br2_Patch.exe after!
After install ePSXe you should set steering.

To make that open ePSXeCutor than click (right button) on the icon of BR2Online, and choose Quick configure
than Options -> Start ePSXe for pad configuring,
than Config -> Game Pad -> Pad 1.


Attention! Don't configurate Pad 2.

To play Bloody Roar 2 Online open ePSXeCutor than double left-click on the icon of BR2Online, find IMG file (default in 'iso' folder in BR2Online directory) and open it.

Then you'll see program which able us to connect with the server.

If you want to create your kaillera server , read the guide at youtube video but only for Local Area Network
Note: Use hamachi and join my room:
Name: pftmclub_server001
pass: pftmclub


To connect your kaillera server,press "Enter IP".. and add your Hamachi IP address..

You have two options to choose: 1) Join (you can join someone else's game) 2) Create a new game (you can start you own game)

Warning:
- Before connecting to the server, you should turn off all the p2p programs and others applications that may cause problems while playing Online.
- If you want to play offline click Cancel in Kaillera
- Remember to not save anything on the Memcard and not enable sound!
- If this sign:
will appear while playing it means that synchronisation was lost and you have to stop the game.
