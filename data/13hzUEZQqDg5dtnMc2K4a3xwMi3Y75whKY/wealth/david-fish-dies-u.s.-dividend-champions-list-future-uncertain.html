David Fish Dies - U.S. Dividend Champions List Future Uncertain

by Prince Humperdinck

Posted on June 19, 2018

Sadly, David Fish, who published the monthly U.S. Dividend Champions list over at dripinvesting.org died on May 12, 2018.

Fish’s spreadsheet formed the backbone of my dividend riches screener and I’d been worried about what would happen to the list when he eventually died.

Justin Law posted an updated spreadsheet on June 4th over at Seeking Alpha. I’m not sure how long he’ll continue to do that.

With some clever web-scraping, we may figure out a way to generate this list for ourselves using Yahoo! Finance (for price/fundamentals) and MarketBeat (to generate the initial list of stocks with 5+ years of increasing dividends).

What will probably take quite a bit of effort is duplicating some of the fancier fields; especially the Confidence Factor, which comprises about 17 components. Perhaps I’ll just drop that one and add as many of its components as reasonably possible.
