Frequent Contributors

Fezzik: A giant among men, Fezzik excels at weightlifting (though he doesn’t even exercise), nursing people back to health, rock-throwing, and crowd-clearing. When not serving on the brute squad, he can often be found fighting gangs for local cherry trees… I mean, charities. Fezzik’s Posts
Prince Humperdinck: Humperdinck’s specialties include, wealth, finance, and tracking a falcon on a cloudy day. And if that sounds hard, try ruling the world someday! Humperdinck’s Posts
Inigo Montoya: The sixth Dread Pirate Roberts, Inigo’s skills include, self-defence, Spanish, cocktails, and rocking a sweet ‘stache. Inigo’s Posts
Miracle Max: Even though the king’s stinking son fired him, ‘Miracle’ Max continues to dabble in the technical arts; the magic of our age. His intrests include the Dark Net, automating the boring stuff, and better living through programming. Max’s Posts
Count Rugen: The six-fingered man. Count Rugen is fascinated by what makes us tick. A man of science, he’s dedicated his life to uncovering the secrets of the body and mind… for posterity. Rugen’s Posts
Vizzini: Ever hear of Socrates, Plato, Aristotle?… Morons! Vizzini is constantly plagued by the fact that he’s a genius living in a world of buffoons. With his ascerbic wit, he provides scathing commentary on the foolishness of what we regular folk do. His annual picnics are the stuff of legend. Vizzini’s Posts
