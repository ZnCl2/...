Fezzik’s Review

Did not like.

I can’t really remember exactly what I didn’t like about it because I just deleted it at some point and never picked it up again. But I think it was probably that the book was very light on theory, principles, and science, and heavy on exercise descriptions and recipies to pad the book. From what I recall, there were little-to-no scientific references; it was all “take my word, folks”.

Description

As an innovator in the field of human performance and training, Nick Tumminello now gives everyone access to his elite training program with Strength Training for Fat Loss. Offering a scientifically based plan for melting fat, Tumminello provides over 150 exercises and nearly 30 ready-to-use workouts to help readers begin transforming their bodies.
