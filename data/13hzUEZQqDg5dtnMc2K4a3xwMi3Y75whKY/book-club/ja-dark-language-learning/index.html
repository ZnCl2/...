Buttercup’s Review

This is a collection of articles by JA-Dark. He had a very scholarly, research-based approach to learning foreign language and wrote articles that focused mainly on using Anki (spaced repetition software) efficiently.

He seems to no-longer be active on the web and most of his articles have disappeared except for on sites such as the Internet Archive.

Description

Good psych info. Focus on Japanese, but mostly relevant to other stuff.
