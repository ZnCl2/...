The Cat of Bubastes

A Tale of Ancient Egypt

★☆☆☆☆

Buttercup’s Review

I was hoping that G.A. Henty’s historical fiction would be an entertaining way to learn a little history. I was dead wrong.

This book was super boring and filled with Henty’s gratuitous promotion of christianity (which didn’t exist in ancient Egypt, so judaism stands in as a sort of proxy).
