[that kind of noise] - [marginal notes], 19th album by [caryoscelus]. CC-BY-SA

a compilation of some of my noisy work during 2019-2021 . most of the tracks are either have been published individually before or simply don't belong anywhere in a bigger picture . still , i tried to make the listen through a coherent and enjoyable experience . there are a few musical videos for these tracks:

  • count misfortune
  • march is coming
  • smoke in the night

[notes]

001 - winter mornings can be depressing with all that air pollution coming down to earth and never ever clearing up

002 - the fortuna leaves a bored count as he goes mad

003 - a few cigarettes are being smoked in the middle of the night . raw tobacco experience

004 - soothing drilling recorded in a bright moment of my life

005 - march was around the corner and the surviving plan was to feed on nothing but grains

006 - a light electronic track featuring stray dogs for vocals

007 - a limited noise collbaration . a music track out of sounds sent by other people https://linuxmusicians.com/viewtopic.php?t=20124

008 - noise of printers , beat of badly installed vents and did somebody bring distortion pedal? - i enjoy the noises of my imaginary office

009 - a movement , or being stale , or just flashes of randomness shining on potential listener

010 - the grand noise machine no one knows how it works or how to operate , but it does resemble a good old organ a little bit
