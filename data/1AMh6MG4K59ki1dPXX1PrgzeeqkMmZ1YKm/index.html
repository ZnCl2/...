Developer Documentation

Why do we need a CDN?

Let's see how many different copies of jQuery you've got in your data folder... each one consumes precious bandwidth and space! Here, it's just stored once: on the ZeroCDN site.

What does this have?

  • jQuery
    • ZeroCDN.js("jquery", "2.1.4", callback)
  • Bootstrap
    • ZeroCDN.js("bootstrap", "3.3.5", callback)
    • ZeroCDN.css("bootstrap", "3.3.5", callback)
  • D3.js
    • ZeroCDN.js("d3", "3.5.6", callback)
