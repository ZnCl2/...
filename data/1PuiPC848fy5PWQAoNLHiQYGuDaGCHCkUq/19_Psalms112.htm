PSALM 112

112:1   Hallelujah! / Blessed is the man who fears Jehovah, / Who delights greatly in His commandments.

112:2   His seed will be mighty on the earth; / The generation of the upright will be blessed.

112:3   Wealth and riches are in his house, / And his righteousness endures forever.

112:4   Light in the darkness arises to the upright; / They are gracious and compassionate and righteous.

112:5   It is well with the man who is gracious and lends; / He will maintain his cause in judgment.

112:6   For he will never be moved; / The righteous man will be remembered forever.

112:7   He will not be afraid of evil tidings; / His heart is steadfast, trusting in Jehovah.

112:8   His heart is sustained, he is not afraid, / Until he sees what should happen to his adversaries.

112:9   He has scattered abroad; he has given to the needy; / His righteousness endures forever; / His horn will be exalted with honor.

112:10  The wicked man will see it and be angry; / He will gnash his teeth and melt away; / The desire of the wicked will perish.
