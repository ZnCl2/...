PSALM 120

120:1   In my distress I called out to Jehovah, / And He answered me.

120:2   O Jehovah, deliver my soul from lying lips, / From a tongue of deceit.

120:3   What should be given to you, / And what further shall be done to you, / O tongue of deceit?

120:4   The sharp arrows of a mighty man, / With the burning coals of the broom tree!

120:5   Woe is me, for I sojourn in Meshech, / I dwell among the tents of Kedar.

120:6   Long has my soul had its dwelling / With him who hates peace.

120:7   I am for peace; but when I speak, / They are for war.
