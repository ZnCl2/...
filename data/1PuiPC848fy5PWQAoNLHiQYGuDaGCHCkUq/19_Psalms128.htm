PSALM 128

128:1   Blessed is everyone who fears Jehovah, / Who walks in His ways.

128:2   You will indeed eat the labor of your hands; / You will be blessed, and it will go well with you.

128:3   Your wife will be like a fruitful vine / In the innermost parts of your house. / Your children will be like olive shoots / Round about your table.

128:4   Thus will the man be blessed / Who fears Jehovah.

128:5   Jehovah bless you from Zion; / And may you see the prosperity of Jerusalem / All the days of your life.

128:6   May you also see the children of your children. / Peace be upon Israel.
