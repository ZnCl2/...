PSALM 149

149:1   Hallelujah! / Sing a new song to Jehovah; / Sing His praise in the congregation of His faithful ones.

149:2   Let Israel rejoice in his Maker; / Let the children of Zion exult in their King.

149:3   Let them praise His name with dancing; / Let them sing psalms to Him with the tambourine and the lyre.

149:4   For Jehovah takes pleasure in His people; / He adorns the lowly with salvation.

149:5   Let the faithful ones exult in glory; / Let them give a ringing shout upon their beds.

149:6   Let the high praises of God be in their throats, / And a two-edged sword in their hand,

149:7   To execute vengeance on the nations / And punishment among the peoples;

149:8   To bind their kings with fetters / And their nobles with chains of iron;

149:9   To execute upon them the judgment written. / This honor is for all His faithful ones. / Hallelujah!
