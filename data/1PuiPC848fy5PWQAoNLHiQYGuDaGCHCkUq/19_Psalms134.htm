PSALM 134

134:1   Bless Jehovah now, / All you servants of Jehovah / Who stand by night in the house of Jehovah.

134:2   Lift up your hands in the sanctuary, / And bless Jehovah.

134:3   May Jehovah, who made heaven and earth, / Bless you from Zion.
