PSALM 87

87:1   His foundation is in the holy mountains.

87:2   Jehovah loves the gates of Zion / More than all the dwellings of Jacob.

87:3   Glorious things are spoken of you, / O city of God. Selah

87:4   I will make mention of Rahab and Babylon as those who know Me; / Behold, Philistia and Tyre along with Cush: / This one was born there, they say.

87:5   But of Zion it will be said, / This one and that one were born in her, / And the Most High Himself will establish her.

87:6   Jehovah will count / When He records the peoples: / This One was born there. Selah

87:7   Then singing as well as dancing, they will say, / All my springs are in you.
