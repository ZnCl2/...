PSALM 116

116:1   I love Jehovah because He hears / My voice, my supplications,

116:2   Because He inclines His ear to me; / Therefore I will call upon Him all my days.

116:3   The bonds of death encompassed me, / And the distresses of Sheol fell upon me; / I fell upon trouble and sorrow.

116:4   But I called upon the name of Jehovah. / O Jehovah, I pray, deliver my soul.

116:5   Jehovah is gracious and righteous, / Indeed our God is compassionate.

116:6   Jehovah preserves the simple: / I was brought low, and He saved me.

116:7   Return to your rest, O my soul; / For Jehovah has dealt bountifully with you.

116:8   For You have delivered my soul from death, / My eyes from tears, / My feet from stumbling.

116:9   I will walk before Jehovah / In the land of the living.

116:10  I believed, therefore I spoke; / I was afflicted greatly.

116:11  I said in my haste, / All men are liars.

116:12  What shall I return to Jehovah / For all His benefits toward me?

116:13  I will take up the cup of salvation / And call upon the name of Jehovah.

116:14  I will pay my vows to Jehovah, / Oh, in the presence of all His people.

116:15  Precious in the sight of Jehovah / Is the death of His faithful ones.

116:16  O Jehovah! / Surely I am Your servant; / I am Your servant, the son of Your female servant; / You have loosed my bonds.

116:17  To You I will offer a sacrifice of thanksgiving, / And I will call upon the name of Jehovah.

116:18  I will pay my vows to Jehovah, / Oh, in the presence of all His people,

116:19  In the courts of the house of Jehovah, / In the midst of you, O Jerusalem. / Hallelujah.
