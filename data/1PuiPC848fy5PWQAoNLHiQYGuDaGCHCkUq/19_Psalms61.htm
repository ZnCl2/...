PSALM 61

61:1   Hear, O God, my cry; / Give heed to my prayer.

61:2   From the end of the earth / I will call out unto You / When my heart faints; / Lead me to the rock that is higher than I.

61:3   For You are a refuge to me, / A strong tower before the enemy.

61:4   Let me sojourn in Your tent forever; / Let me take refuge in the covering of Your wings. Selah

61:5   For You, O God, have heard my vows; / You have given me the inheritance of those who fear Your name.

61:6   You will add days to the days of the king; / May his years be like generation after generation.

61:7   May he dwell forever before God; / Appoint lovingkindness and faithfulness, that they may preserve him.

61:8   So I will sing psalms to Your name forever, / As I repay my vows day by day.
