PSALM 133

133:1   Behold, how good and how pleasant it is / For brothers to dwell in unity!

133:2   It is like the fine oil upon the head / That ran down upon the beard, / Upon Aaron’s beard, / That ran down upon the hem of his garments;

133:3   Like the dew of Hermon / That came down upon the mountains of Zion. / For there Jehovah commanded the blessing: / Life forever.
