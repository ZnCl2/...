PSALM 150

150:1   Hallelujah! / Praise God in His sanctuary; / Praise Him in the expanse that manifests His power.

150:2   Praise Him for His mighty acts; / Praise Him according to His vast greatness.

150:3   Praise Him with the blast of the trumpet; / Praise Him with the harp and lyre;

150:4   Praise Him with the tambourine and with dancing; / Praise Him with the stringed instrument and the pipe.

150:5   Praise Him with the loud cymbals; / Praise Him with the loud clanging cymbals.

150:6   Let everything that has breath praise Jehovah. / Hallelujah!
