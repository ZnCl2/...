Immortal Religion

Samsara the endless cycle of rebirth, some people try to escape it. Personally I think life is captivating, educational and entertaining. As Stuart Wilde said “Life was never meant to be a struggle”.

With in-between-life regression scientific research, we know that you can stay in the soul world as long as you want, but the fun and intense learning experiences are happening here in the galaxy cosmos or incarnated space.

Immortality is optional, if you don’t want to remember your past-lives, or have continuity with your future lives, so be it. Having a blank slate is essential or at least very helpful for some lessons.

However for those of us, who may benefit from more continuity, that option is also available. It is something we can provide for ourselves and each other.

There are many reasons one may wish to remember their past-lives, including to heal from past-life traumas, to gain a greater understanding of self, and to gain wisdom that is helpful in your current life.

For those approaching the end of their bodies life, an option we may be able to make available is reincarnation locating services.
