Reincarnation

Reincarnation locating services

A good introduction to the current state of the art of reincarnation locating services is covered in the documentary “Unmistaken Child”.

Essential to understanding how it works, is knowing about Ludic Fallacy, which is propagated by some Nihlists, who claim that things happen at random, like the rolling of dice in a game. That would be true if the things that happened were closed systems, but here on Earth, and indeed the galaxy cosmos we have an open-system where we affect each other, and things are inter-related.

  1. The direction of the smoke at cremation time is used to see the general direction in which someone may reincarnate.
  2. A channelled oracle may be consulted for some information.
  3. A form of divination can be used, to ascertain the probabilities of various variables in relation to the reincarnation destination, including place and parent names.
  4. Visions may occur to people close to the person that is to reincarnate, particularly when it is near the time of their reincarnation.
  5. Once it is determined that they have reincarnated and a rough age is available a search ensues in the area where they are most likely to be.
  6. The children are tested by seeing if they are familiar with the clothing and personal items of the former host body.
  7. Formal testing of the former host body objects from a variety of alternative objects of the same type but different in appearance can take place where there is a high statistical probability of failure. And thus a high confidence rating if they are successful.
  8. Additional testing may be viable with past life regression, to see if their past life memories line up with those of the previous incarnation(s).

While ideally we would have the co-operation of Tibetan Buddhism, as they have much experience in this regard and take it quite seriously. If not it is possible to do indpendantly.

Financial

Obviously this would be one of the most expensive procedures, and as such would likely initially only be available to the rich and those who have enough people willing to volunteer and donate resources (such as Buddhist Rinpoches).

People going this method will still be encouraged to give 60\% of their inheritance to their current bodies surviving relations to avoid envy problems. The rest can go to a trust fund for their future incarnation, and to pay for the service itself.

In the event that they don’t reincarnate on Earth or an accessible planet, there should also be designated a charity or place or person the trust fund will go to support.
