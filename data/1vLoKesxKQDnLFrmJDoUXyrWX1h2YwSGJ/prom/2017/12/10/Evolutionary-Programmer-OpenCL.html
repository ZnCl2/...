Evolutionary Programmer: OpenCL and TODO

December 10, 2017

OpenCL integration turned out to be more of a headache than I had initially suspected.

Debugging OpenCL

I was getting many errors that were wildly fluctuating with small numerical changes. This I eventually managed to narrow down to some includes, that I had for making vector operations efficient on CPU, which weren’t necessary for the GPU, so after taking out those includes that was mostly fixed.

Though I continued to have errors, primarily it seems to be because I was attempting to integrate it through a live GPU, and the program is rather large. I did eventually get it to work by using it via POCL or Portable OpenCL, that conforms to the OpenCL2.0 standard, and doesn’t have the issues GPU hardware does at times.
