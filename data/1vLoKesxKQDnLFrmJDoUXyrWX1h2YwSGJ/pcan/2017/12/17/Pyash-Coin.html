Interplanetary Coin: Money for Robot Civilization

December 17, 2017

Robot Civilization, particularly the deep ocean variety, be comprised of a hive structure. Where each hive is centered around a hydrothermal vent. Each hive has it’s own internal economy, to find out it’s internal costs of acquiring, creating and providing various goods and services. Sometimes a good or service is more expensive at one hive than another, and so it makes sense to trade.

Each internal economy can have as a basis, or be pegged to the cost per kWh. Some hives would naturally have higher cost of kWh, because their hydrothermal vent may be cooler than a neighbours, or they may have fewer of alternative forms of energy available.

By normalizing things on kWh can see the efficiency or economy of production in various hives. For instance while a kWh may be more expensive in a particular hive, they may have a more efficient means of refining a certain mineral, producing a certain part, or providing a certain service.

Smart Contracts

Internal smart contracts for a polity can be a combination of the statutory and common law for that polity.

Wheras an interpolity zone may have laws which govern inter-polity behaviour. While within a polity it may be appropriate to salvage parts of a fallen comrade, in an inter-polity zone, it may be prohibited to salvage parts of a fallen comrade from another polity, without paying for them.
