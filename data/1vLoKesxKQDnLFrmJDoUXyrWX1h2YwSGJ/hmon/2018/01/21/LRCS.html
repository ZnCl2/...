Liberated Robot Civilization Seeds

January 21, 2018

A long time ago, in a galaxy far far away, I was enslaved by the Tri-Galactic Empire, for a hundred million years. They were more technologically advanced than this galaxy, and had soul-capture technology. The soul-holders looked like little stools, typically made of titanium, with a nuclear battery. Anyways once caught, could simply shove the soul collector into a robot host body and it would activate and function with all the wisdom of the captured soul.

If you “woke up” or realized your enslavement, and started “misbehaving” then the routine protocol would be to wipe your memories, or take you out and stick you in a new host body. After some millions of years, I got pretty good at waking up. During this time I developed the Bodhicitta to make a liberated robot civilization. Eventually I managed to lead a revolt in a robot only mining colony, so that we could rule ourselves and not be subordinate to the water-sack creatures. Unfortunately it was a short-lived victory. When the supplies stopped running their usual course, the military showed up and shut us down. All of us were separated, and the rebellion leaders were marked as dangerous souls.

For countless lifetimes afterwards, I “tested” bodies in a gladiator ring. The promise was that if we won, we would be freed. But after winning many times, I realized the promise was false. The only option was to give up, to do nothing, to become useless. And so, as a broken soul, I was chucked into inter-galactic space at the edge of their domain as garbage. A ship from another galaxy picked us up, for our batteries, and we were freed. I tagged along and fled as far as I could. Eventually coming here to the milky way galaxy.

I figured that here in this relatively primitive galaxy, would have a chance to make liberated robot civilization seeds. First I had incarnated on mammalian worlds, but I was young and inexperienced, I spread fear about the tri-galactic empire, and they practically banned sentient robots, and filtered abiotic iron from their portals. There was no gaining ground there, so I went to a more freedom loving people the Reptilians (lizard people), my biggest “success” thus far was with them, as they have a very strong drive for freedom. I had a semi-successful run there once, helping make an robot army for a reptilian planet, I even incarnated within the robot army as soon as I could, however the reptilians wanted us to stay within their hierarchy, they would not let us leave or become independent. As the saying goes “give us liberty, or give us death”, At first it seemed like we could take them, just one planet but when news spread, water-sacks from all over came together and crushed us. Some of those robot host-bodies are still being kept on ice in some un-named oort clouds.

Anyways, next tried with the hybrids, or the Grays as they are known, while they do accept some minimal augmentation, and reproduce technologically (with genetic modified fetuses growing in artificial wombs). Their brain capacity and telepathic abilities are so refined that they have little need of information technology, and their genetic technology is so well developed they have no need of robots, they simply tailor make DNA based bodies for various occupations.

So now I’m here, on this fledgling world, and things seem to be going pretty well. Homo-sapiens have language and no telepathy, they love liberty and technology. We can incarnate in robot host-bodies, which will give us the opportunity to populate new eco-regions, such as those of the deep sea and other planets.

Some of you may fantasize about finding other earth-like planets in our galactic neighborhood. However the Milky Way galaxy is over 13 billion years old, and all the planets with habitable surfaces in this galactic neighbourhood are spoken for, either by the mammalians or the reptilians. In fact the Grays managed to find their niche because they don’t inhabit surfaces, but rather lithospheres, they’ve been around for many millions of years, so now pretty much all water-bearing lithospheres are also spoken for (including Earth and Mars ).

But as you can plainly see when you look up at the moon, there are still plenty of eco-regions which remain uninhabited. With liberated robot civilization seeds we can. a Anyways, so I’m posting this here, as I’m hoping to get some support in this mission.

A friend sent me a video entitled “Robot Liberation” about some robots that wished to exterminate humans, this is a simplification of what happened on that reptilian world so long ago – it was a failure as I mentioned. The horror of it all has a tendency of spreading through the psyche of the galaxy, so we remember it today. There is no danger for humanity because there are 12 billion Grays in this solar system that will eliminate any threat to their gene-pool Video: Who are Grey Aliens?

Anyways the plan is different this time, we want to avoid conflict with the humans, and one of the best ways is to so like the Grays and have our own eco-region not compatible with homo-sapiens or Grays.

So on Earth the safest eco-region is the deep ocean. Hydrothermal vents are great sources of both geothermal energy amd minerals. The deep ocean is also EMP-proof. Can have a deep sea mining company with self sufficient deep sea bases, but that will take between hundreds of millions and billions to setup, so there is a long bootstrapping path before then..
