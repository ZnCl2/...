Sea colony

Profit Strategy

Deep sea mining, refining and manufacturing, using hydrothermal vents and geothermally derived electricity.

By not having to elevator raw ore, can significantly reduce cost in comparison to other deep sea mining companies.

Hydrothermal vents

There are lots of hydrothermal vents in nature, mainly lining geologically active areas such as platectonic boundaries.

Hydrothermal vents can also theoretically be created in a similar manner to how geothermal power is derived, by drilling inwells and outwells

Pitcairn Islands

The Pitcairn Islands are a remote set of volcanic islands in the South Pacific. They have at least one hydrothermal vent in their vicinity.

Because they have a low population, they should be happy to support deep sea mining operations in their area.
