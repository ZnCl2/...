Planned Communities

An alternative corporate structure made possible because of Pyash deliberative democracy.

Profit Strategy

People would have ideal scenario for achieving self-actualization, many self-actualization ideas may be profitable. The originator would get a percentage of the profit and have the companies sizeable resources to help make it a reality.

Different Culture

One of the first mover disadvantages is training staff, that is then hired up by competitors. By having a different culture, it may be possible to make it relatively difficult for a competitor to meet the needs of employees, which are used to working ~2 hours a day, having a steady paycheck, and plenty of family time, social and spirtual group events, possibly intentional communities, that make even a small income from us, worth more than one nth times greater from a SAC (Standard American Corporation) culture.

For instance, technically most humans only need a fairly small income to meet their basic needs, of food, water and shelter. An intentional community can provide safety, security and belongingness. Prestige can be gained by doing well in work, or helping people in the community. Self-actualization can be achieved through creative pioneering in any of the many areas that are necessary for successful robot civilization seeds.

People will be able to start and maintain families in the communities (with between 1 and ~200 people), as that is part of the cosmist research. So all the family members and children can be paid for the services and data they provide to the company.

There is also the alternative that people could have this steady salary, and not be in a community. Perhaps they want to live a nomadic existence, that is fully acceptable as well. Only have to work 9 months of the year, between 1 and 4 hours per weekday. Very few companies would be willing to allow for such a thing.

We can ascertain whay the employee/contractor wants in their ideal life during the interview process. And of course they can change their mind later on and try out different lifestyles which may be available.

The goal is not so much to hire a few people and pay them huge quantities, but rather to hire a large number of people, and help them have a good quality life.
