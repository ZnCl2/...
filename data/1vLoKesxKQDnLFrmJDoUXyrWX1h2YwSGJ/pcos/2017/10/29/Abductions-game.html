(Alien) Abductions game

October 29, 2017

I got a video game idea. I wanted to bounce it off you, cause I don’t play games lol. Anyways I read this http://nypost.com/2017/10/26/man-desperately-trying-to-sell-his-alien-infested-ranch/, where some guy cut up some Grays with a katana and one about how some single-person game thing in vancouver got shut down cause people want to play games as a service.

Okay, so my idea is for a game called “Abductions”, and in it a person wakes up as a human with a headache/feeling-tired, with some evidence of being abducted. They are a UFO researcher. and their goal is to find out about their abduction experiences.

Once they get enough info, and-or infiltrate a gray alien base, they can choose to wake up as a men-in-black or a gray-alien, depending on what story line they followed. it can be multi-player with advanced players filling in the Grays and stuff which are initially NPC’s.

There can be a thick-headedness rating for how hard it is for the Gray-hypnotists to entrance someone. so someone really thick-headed might be able to kill a few with a katana, or they could wear some telepathy-proof hat or w/e.

If they manage to get killed by a Gray, they also get to wake up as one. kinda like world of warcraft, except it has Grays instead of orcs. they live underground and need to abduct humans to reproduce. not sure what to make the goal of the humans though….

The game world can keep updating as new intel or stories come out from abductees. so it’ll be game as a service

Oh I know, people could wake up in various situations of real abductees, so like that have been reported by abductees, that way can also increase the number of humans can wake up as over time, game as a service style.

Men in black are tasked with keeping the peace between aliens and humans, mostly by silencing people with a negative opinion about aliens. Behind the scenes they are allied with Grays.

human stats could include a bar for friendliness to aliens, fame, and knowledge. natural thick-headedness is inversely proportional to intelligence, so it puts a cap on knowledge, so unlikely to join the Grays, but can join the men-in-black.

The grays would have temperature bar since they are cold blooded, if it gets too cold their dexterity and hypnotic ability diminishes.

when a person is being abducted by NPC’s they just get a summary after of any symptoms. but if they are successfully abducted by a playing character, then they can read up on alien material or play some mini-games for a chance to wake up during the abduction.

If game is popular enough can include Mars, where people can wake up as mars colonist, mars men in black, or mars aliens.

So initially game can start with just one alien base, modeled of the one in dulce, new mexico. where the aliens live at the bottom, men in black in the middle, and humans are in a small town, or rural area nearby.

Can expand based on game usage, such as having a peurto-rico underwater base, a lunar base, or ones where the grays are more reptilian, or more humanoid.

In an expansion, can also include the Pleadians, which are actually Nazi’s based in Antarctica masquerading as aliens. Then could have political figure-heads like conservative fascists backed by the Pleadians and liberal socialists backed by Grays.
