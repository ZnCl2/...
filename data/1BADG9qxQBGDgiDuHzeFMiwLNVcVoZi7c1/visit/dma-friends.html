DMA Friends

Thank you for being a DMA Friend!

The DMA Friends program ended on December 31, 2017, so that we can focus on new ways for you to enjoy your visits. The DMA will continue to offer free general admission every day during Museum hours, and for many special exhibitions.

​Redemption of DMA Friends points for rewards is no longer allowed.

Stay active with the Museum by

  • Becoming a DMA Member and enjoying a full year of exclusive benefits and privileges, including FREE parking in the Museum's garage, FREE tickets to all special exhibitions, FREE access to every Late Night and Second Thursdays with a Twist, and more!
  • Updating your email preferences to stay in the know about all of the exciting programs and events at the DMA.

Questions? Please contact the DMA Members team at 214-922-1347 or email members@DMA.org.

 

DMA Friends was a FREE loyalty program promoting participation and engagement with art for DMA Members and all visitors to the Museum!
