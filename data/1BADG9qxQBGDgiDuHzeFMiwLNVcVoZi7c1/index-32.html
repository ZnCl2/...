Welcome to Ko Awatea LEARN
Learn your way

Ko Awatea LEARN is a health sector focused, eLearning and education community with a wide range of programmes, courses, and community forums. Currently LEARN has 15 DHB partners across New Zealand and many of these DHBs are facilitating access to their resources to their PHOs, NGOs and primary care partners.

Its goal is to create a collaborative environment for DHB staff, and the wider health sector, to continue their learning journey, helping develop a safe, competent and compliant workforce.
