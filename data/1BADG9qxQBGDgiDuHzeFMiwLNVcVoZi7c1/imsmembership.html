How to Join IMS

Become a Part of the Education Technology Innovation Revolution!

IMS Global is legally organized as a non-profit member organization. IMS depends on membership dues to exist and spread. But, more importantly, IMS exists to serve the direction that the members want to go!

Support the cause and reap the benefits! Your organization should become a member of IMS Global, regardless of size or whether a supplier or institution because:

Technical Resources

Members have access to unparalled technical resources that accelerate implementation and reduce cost/time to market.

 

Member Network

Members have access to the IMS network of education segment-leading organizations who frequently partner to lead the sector forward.

 

Sector Leadership

IMS Global members are leading the revolution and perceived as the world leaders among EdTech institutions and suppliers

 

IMS Global Has Three Membership Levels

Contributing Member Affiliate Member Alliance Member

Voting members of IMS providing leadership to any and all IMS initiatives and technical work

Non-voting development network-only participation across all IMS technical work
