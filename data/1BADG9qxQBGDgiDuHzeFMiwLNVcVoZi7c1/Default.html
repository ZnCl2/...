The Volunteer Centre BWF is your gateway to volunteering, for individuals and volunteer-involving organisations (VIOs). We are a Volunteering England accredited Centre, member of the National Council for Voluntary Organisations (NCVO), and are one of only a handful of organisations across the region with the nationally recognised Investing in Volunteers (IIV) standard.

 

If you need expert support in volunteer management rest assured we have fully accredited Investing in Volunteers (IIV) Advisors to support you - in fact we have 2 of only 15 trained advisors in the country!

 

We offer a range of support and training for volunteers and are an approved AQA and RSPH Centre.

 

We are proud that our work and experience has led to the creation of our Volunteer Academy model and passport which is offered as tailored programme for organisations and has been built into Blackpool's fantastic Better Start Programme for families in Blackpool.

 

We are members of and particpate in an array of local, regional and national groups to ensure we are a voice for volunteers and volunteer management and are home to the Volunteer Charter!

 

Our core functions:

 

1.    Strategic development of volunteering

 

2.    Good practise development

 

3.    Developing volunteer opportunities

 

4.    Voice of volunteering

 

5.    Brokerage
