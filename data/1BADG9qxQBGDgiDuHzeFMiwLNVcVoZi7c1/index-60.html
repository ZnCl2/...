    Prior Learning Assessment

    Save time and money. Earn college credit for what you already know, then apply it toward completion of your undergraduate degree.

    Earn Credits

    Approximately 50% of undergraduate students receive prior learning assessment (PLA) credits when they enroll at SUNY Empire.

    Save Time

    When you earn credits from accredited institutions or evaluated professional learning, you cut your time-to-degree by as much as three semesters.

    Save Money

    PLA credits from SUNY Empire can save you an average of $5,891 on your undergraduate degree.
